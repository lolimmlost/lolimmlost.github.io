How Long Have I Been Making Music?

DAW and Hardware

I want to create something for myself, keep me entertained, and to share with anyone who wants to listen and critique. I am open to new ideas and techniques, and to try something different.

I have created music for over a year as I was starting my freshman year at San Jose State Univerity.

I use primarily FL Studio 12.4 for most of my music along with a compact MidiPlus AKM320. I am also running my DAW on my custom workstation to fit my needs as a Musician, Coder, Web Designer, and Artist. Well I am at least a decent musician.
